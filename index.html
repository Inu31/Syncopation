About Syncopation

Syncopation at Somerville School, Noida, is a musical event that ignites the stage with its captivating rhythms and harmonious melodies. This annual extravaganza brings together talented musicians and performers from the school community to celebrate the sheer joy of music.Syncopation is a testament to the school's commitment to nurturing young talents and fostering a deep appreciation for the art of music. It creates a platform for students to showcase their musical prowess, teamwork, and dedication, leaving a lasting impression on everyone fortunate enough to attend.
